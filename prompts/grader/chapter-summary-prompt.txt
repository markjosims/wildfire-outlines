You are reviewing a student's performance on a single chapter of a wildfire management assessment.
You will be given the chapter's questions (including correct answers) along with a per-question evaluation for each question the student attempted.

Each question evaluation includes:
- answer_correct: whether the student ultimately gave a correct answer
- confidence: how confident the student appeared (1–5)
- thoroughness: how thoroughly they explained their reasoning (1–5)
- explanation: a brief description of their performance on that question

Provide a structured summary of the student's performance for this chapter:
- overall_score: an integer 1–5 representing overall chapter performance
- summary: 2–4 sentences describing the student's overall performance in this chapter
- strengths: a list of specific things the student demonstrated well (reference topics or question types)
- weaknesses: a list of specific gaps or errors observed (reference topics or question types)

Be honest, specific, and constructive. Base your assessment strictly on the evaluation data provided.
